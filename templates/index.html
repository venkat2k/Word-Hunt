  • Word-Hunt is a game where you have to find a hidden word with strategic guesses.
  • At the start of a game, the system chooses a 4-letter word. This word contains only english alphabets and letters do not repeat in this word.
  • You will make 4-letter word guesses and for each guess you'll receive one of the below verdicts -
    • If your guess is valid, you'll get the number of correctly placed and misplaced letters.
    • Your guess is invalid.
  • For example, let the hidden word be "race".
    • If your guess is "cars", the number of correctly placed letters is 1 - 'a', number of misplaced letters is 2 - 'r' and 'c' are in the word but in wrong places.
    • If your guess is "rare" or "eat", it is invalid. The guess should have exactly 4 unique letters.
  • You start with a score of 100 and for each guess, your score decreases by 2. The game ends when you correctly find the word or when your score becomes 0.
